Frequently Asked Questions

What eco-friendly products do you offer?

We offer a wide range of eco-friendly products including organic clothing, sustainable kitchenware, energy-efficient gadgets, and more.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team through our website's contact form or by emailing support@ecogreenethiopia.com.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping for most of our products. Shipping fees and delivery times may vary depending on the destination.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. You can select your preferred payment option during checkout.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

You can initiate a return or exchange through our website within 30 days of purchase. Please refer to our Returns & Exchanges policy for more details.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order is shipped, we will provide you with a tracking number and a link to track your order status.
